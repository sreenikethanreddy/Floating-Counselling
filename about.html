Meet Our Founder

"Resilience And Self Confidence Saved My Life"

Celestina vowed to never have children because of fear of repeating trauma she experienced from her stepmother... At 16, she suffered a spinal injury that left her paralysed for almost 24 hours. She taught herself to walk again, despite doctors telling her she may never walk.

This experience awakened a deep love of life and passion to work with children. She is now a wife and mother of 2.

As a fully certified and qualified Integrative, Behaviour Psychotherapist with 15 years in the profession, her mission is to help parents heal their past traumas so they do not pass them on to their children.
