Property Services

Guaranteed Rent & Management

Floating Counselling Property offers property management services run by an experienced landlord, aimed at making life easier for estate agents and landlords.

We handle everything from tenant sourcing to minor maintenance, so you can enjoy a stress-free income stream with guaranteed monthly rent.

  • Guaranteed rent every month for 3–7 years
  • Professional tenants (NHS workers, bankers, etc.)
  • Minor maintenance handled (under £200)
  • Weekly cleaners and grocery deliveries for tenants
  • No voids, no marketing, zero stress

Registered with Property Redress Scheme (PRS) & Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
