Why families stay with us

One organisation, many layers of support

Healing is rarely just one appointment. People need safe conversations, practical options, trusted relationships and a place where dignity is protected. That is the gap Floating Counselling Community is built to fill.

  • Therapeutic care for individuals, couples, families and groups.
  • Community-based support for housing pressure, food insecurity and wellbeing.
  • Programmes for parents, children and young people that translate insight into daily action.
