Empowering Community

Floating Counselling Community is a UK-based grassroots organisation dedicated to empowering individuals, families, and marginalised groups through holistic support, therapeutic care, and community-driven programmes.

Inspired by Maslow's hierarchy of needs, we believe in meeting people where they are, emotionally, practically, and socially, to create lasting change and personal growth that is a Wraparound Care. We are proud to be led by a diverse team of African women, whose lived experience and professional expertise shape every part of our work.

Therapy With Realistic Tools

We have combined 45 years as clinical Psychotherapists with 50,000+ clinical hours to help individuals, children, and parents to live better and be better with realistic tools and strategies that have a proven scientific record.

Finding it hard to understand or manage your child's behaviour? We Can Help. Even if therapy hasn't worked before, it will with us, provided you are willing to commit to the tasks given by your fully qualified Psychotherapist.

Not For You If...
