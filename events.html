Current community-facing work

Homeless Necessity — The Woodside Project & Advocacy Portal

Responding to London's homelessness crisis by going out to speak directly with rough sleepers, distributing donated clothes, food, and essentials. Providing therapeutic support and property signposting for those facing housing displacement.
