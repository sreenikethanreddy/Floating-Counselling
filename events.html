Community Events

A history of our impactful local and international programmes bringing people together.

Historic Milestones

From outdoor cinemas to anti-knife crime education, our reach spans specific community needs across the years.

Partnership

Croydon Central East (CrESNT)

Delivering a locality project for residents via a dedicated Local Community Partnership fund.

2022

Croydon Outdoor Cinema

Blockbuster movies free to all residents in partnership with Mother of Nations. Included luxury hampers, health/wellbeing education, and refreshments.

June 2022

Community Park Party

A 7-hour alcohol-free family event at Woodside Green celebrating the Queen's Platinum Jubilee, complete with food and activities.

2019

Homeless Necessity Project

Responding to London's homelessness crisis by going out to speak directly with rough sleepers, distributing donated clothes, food, and essentials.

2019

Anti-Knife Crime Project

Educating young people in schools on the real consequences of knife crime and carrying weapons.

2016 – 2017

Women's Monthly Pampering

A focused programme combining physical health, mental wellbeing, education, empowerment, and much-needed self-care.

2017

Business Retreat

A weekend getaway at Hilton Hotel, Cobham. Specifically designed for entrepreneurs and business owners to network and achieve personal growth.

International Community Support

Our impact extends far beyond the UK. Through dedicated international outreach programmes, especially focused on Africa, we aim to provide resources, business support, and community aid in international market settings. We believe wraparound care is a global necessity.
