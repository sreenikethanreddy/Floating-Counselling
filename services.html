Therapy Principles

What our work is designed to do

  • Help clients be aware of their healing journey
  • Work through past hurts, traumas and let downs
  • Prevent generational trauma cycles
  • Teach emotional regulation and resilience
  • Improve communication in relationships
  • Create positive attachment between parent and child

What we do not do

"We do not expect or encourage you to behave as your counsellor would behave if confronted with a similar problem in their own life."

Our outcome goal is to decrease maladaptive behaviour, build long-term resilience, and create healthy secure attachments.
