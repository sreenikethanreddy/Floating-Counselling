Holiday School

Combining academic enrichment with emotional intelligence and therapy for children aged 5–11.

Therapeutic Care Meets Fun

Our Holiday School isn't just about activities; it's a structural programme integrating academic maths and science, healthy eating education ("You & Food Matter"), and deep emotional intelligence development.

We teach core values: Honesty, Fairness, Kindness, Integrity, and Being Outspoken.

The Team Behind the Magic

  • Fitness Instructors
  • Professional Bankers (Financial Literacy)
  • Actresses and Singers (Creativity)

Example Weekly Themes

Carefully structured four-week therapeutic focuses.

Week 1: Hope & Dreams

"My dreams and hopes are reachable." Children learn they have choices and practice happiness visualization and hopefulness.

Week 2: Uniqueness

"It's okay to ask for help." Focusing on the beauty of why we are unique and making better choices with friends.

Week 3: Confidence

Affirmation and mirror work. Building deep self-love, confidence, and emotional intelligence.

Week 4: Coping with Loss

Soul work and appreciation. Writing letters for loved ones and exploring healthy ways of coping with endings.
