Holiday School

Therapeutic care, creativity and confidence for children aged 5 to 11.

Our holiday programme blends enrichment, emotional intelligence and joyful learning so children grow academically, socially and personally.

What children experience

Maths, science, healthy eating, creativity, emotional development and values-based learning in one structured environment.

Programme Design

Therapeutic care meets fun

Our Holiday School integrates academic maths and science, healthy eating education and emotional intelligence development. Children are encouraged to grow through honesty, fairness, kindness, integrity and the confidence to speak up.

The team behind the magic

  • Professional psychotherapists and artists
  • Electrical engineers supporting science learning
  • Fitness instructors and creative practitioners
  • Bankers teaching financial literacy
  • Actresses and singers nurturing creativity

Weekly Themes

Carefully structured four-week focuses

Week 1: Hope and dreams

Children learn that their hopes are reachable and practice happiness visualisation and hopeful thinking.

Week 2: Uniqueness

Children explore the beauty of what makes them unique, including asking for help and making better friendship choices.

Week 3: Confidence

Affirmation work, mirror exercises and self-love practices help build confidence and emotional intelligence.
