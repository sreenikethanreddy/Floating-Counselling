What You'll Learn

This book is your toolkit for conscious, loving discipline. Based on decades of clinical psychotherapy experience working with families from all backgrounds.

🧠

Emotional Regulation

About the Author

Celestina Oniye-Thomas is a fully certified Integrative Behaviour Psychotherapist with 15 years of clinical experience. As the founder of Floating Counselling, her mission is to help parents heal their past traumas and create nurturing environments for their children.
